Our Specialized Cabinet Services

✓ Disaster Restoration & Reconstruction

When a disaster restoration company brings us damaged kitchen or bathroom cabinet boxes, faceframes, or related hardware, we take over the meticulous rebuild process. We are experts in building new boxes and shelves, and precisely installing all drawers, doors, and hardware. This ensures a seamless transition from damaged structure to fully functional unit.

  • Finish Matching: We stain, paint, or lacquer match existing finishes to create new skins, fillers, toekick, and trim.
  • Installation: We offer professional, start-to-finish cabinet installation services.
  • Specialty Styles: Our team is skilled in handling European/faceless/commercial style cabinets and custom requirements.

✓ Custom Cabinet Ordering & Design

Beyond restoration, we are authorized to order new cabinets and accessories from multiple different, high-quality cabinet manufacturers. This gives homeowners unparalleled choice for new construction or remodels.

  • In-Home Consultation: We go out to measure up the house ourselves for maximum accuracy.
  • Custom Floorplans: We draft custom floorplans tailored to the specific dimensions and flow of the home.
  • Client Choice: Homeowners select their preferred cabinet style, wood species, and paint or stain color, ensuring their vision is perfectly realized.
